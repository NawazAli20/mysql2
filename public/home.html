Peoria has a humid continental climate with hot, humid summers and cold winters.

Population

Peoria has a population of approximately 115,000 residents.

Sports

Peoria is home to several professional and collegiate sports teams, including the Peoria Rivermen (IHL), the Peoria Pride (NBA D-League), and various collegiate teams.
